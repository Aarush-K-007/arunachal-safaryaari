Top Destinations

Some of the most untouched landscapes in all of India.

Tawang

West Arunachal · 3500m+

Home to one of the world's largest Buddhist monasteries. Dramatic mountain passes, frozen lakes, and Tibetan culture.

Monastery Snow Trek

Ziro Valley

Lower Subansiri

Terraced rice fields, Apatani villages, and excellent birding. UNESCO World Heritage tentative site.

Culture Birding

Namdapha

Changlang

India's third-largest national park — home to all four big cats. 800+ bird species. Deep jungle camping.

Wildlife Jungle Camp

Mechuka

A rarely visited valley near the China border. Ancient monastery, river camp, and Tibetan-flavoured landscapes.

About Us

Arunachal Safaryaari is a small, local travel company based in New Delhi and Itanagar. We are guides, naturalists, and storytellers — people who grew up in this land.

  • Inner Line Permits: We handle all ILP paperwork. Included in every package.
  • Local Guides Only: Every trek is led by someone from that district.
  • Responsible Travel: No plastic in protected areas. We partner with tribal homestays.
  • Small Groups: Max 8 people per tour for a quieter, more personal experience.
